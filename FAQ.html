FAQ

How do I register መጠይቅ

Registering on መጠይቅ is FREE and easy for individual accounts.

Simply scroll to the top of our homepage and click on the “Sign Up” button. During the registration process you will be requested to provide some information about yourself. Eg. Name, email address, country of residence, etc.

Without a valid email address, it will not be possible to register on the መጠይቅ site since your email address is used as an account number with us. You will also be required to create a password for yourself.

Is መጠይቅ Service Free?

Depending on your account, for individual accounts it is free but for a corporation there are certain fees related to use of our platform.

If you are an existing user, go to the home page, and at the top right corner of the page, click on the “Log In“ icon and type your user name and password which you have used upon creating መጠይቅ account and then click on the “Log In’ icon.

If you don’t have an account, go to the home page, and at the top right of the page, click on the “Sign up” icon, get started by filling out your email address and password. And then click on the “Sign up” icon after filling out the details.

I have forgotten my password - how can I access my account?

Can’t remember your password? Need another one? Don’t worry. Resetting your password is a very simple process.

On the homepage, click on “Login” which will redirect you to the login box. From there, click on the “Forgot Password?” You will be once again redirected to a password recovery page, which will require you to enter the email address you used to register at መጠይቅ.

You will receive an email to the same address instructing you how to reset your password. However, please be aware that we can only send a password reminder to you if you have previously registered an email address and password with us.

How do I search for available surveys?

After you have created your user account and filled out the neccessary information, surveys related to your profile will be sent to you from corporations listed under our website.

Alternatively, if you want a more refined search you can browse the different catagories of surveys located just below the search bar.

Will I need to download any software to use መጠይቅ?

No, መጠይቅ does not require any software, other than an Internet browser, to be purchased or downloaded onto your computer to use our service.
